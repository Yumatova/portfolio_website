Money Jar

About

Money jar is a mobile app that helps save money for students or people who are learning how to be financially educated. App allows users to track expenses and general money flow by connecting to the bank account. The interface itself is clean and intuitive. Financial adviser that’s always in your pocket.

Problem Statement

Sometimes it can be complicated to put some money aside and save it for a big goal, this tool is making life easier and helps to get to your desired point sooner.
