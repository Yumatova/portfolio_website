My Story

Hello there!I’m Viktoriia, UX/UI designer from Los Angeles with a background in fine art and illustrations. I’m also a co-founder of kid’s art studio “Art is fun” in Chicago city. I’ve had an amazing experience working with people, that is provided me with organizational skills, good time management, and problem-solving tenor. As an artist, I got an eye for an aesthetic, intuitive sense of color, and well-balanced composition. Motivated by curiosity,decided to switch to digital art and UI itself. Ready to apply all my knowledge to make a strong cohesive interface based on user's experience and bring positive impact on people's lives,by making digital products easier and more intuitive to use. When I'm not designing I like to travel. One of a little-big goals visit all 50 states in the US and start exploring the world! By the way, I've been to 13 states already! As my work requires a lot of time next to the screen, on my days off I prefer to do as many activities outside as I can: hiking, swimming, dancing, and snorkeling. Catch me anywhere but home!
